About the Model

This ElasticNet regression model combines L1 and L2 regularization to predict social media engagement. It analyzes various factors including platform, post type, timing, and trending status to provide accurate predictions.

  • Platform: Different social media platforms have varying engagement patterns
  • Content Type: Videos and polls tend to generate higher engagement
  • Timing: Hour, day of week, and seasonal patterns affect visibility
  • Trending Status: Content alignment with trends impacts reach
